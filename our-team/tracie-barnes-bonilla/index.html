Our Team Tracie Barnes Bonilla

Tracie Barnes Bonilla

Director of Administration

Tracie Barnes Bonilla is the Director of Administration for Sam Hill Tree Care. She joined the team in 2019. With more than 25 years of financial, accounting and administrative experience, Tracie is an incredible asset to the organization. With her guidance, the administrative team plays an essential role in delivering the unparalleled service that Sam Hill Tree Care is known for.

Tracie earned her bachelors degree in Accounting from the University of Memphis and serves as a Texas State Notary. She has continued her knowledge of Business through various training courses including Mulvaney Management Training, and Aileron’s Professional Management System training as part of the company’s initiative for continuous improvement and education.

When not in the office, Tracie enjoys spending time with her family, visiting her daughter Alyssa at the University of Oklahoma and catching the occasional Sooners game. She also enjoys DIY projects and traveling. Her family attends Lake Pointe Church of Rockwall and she actively volunteers with their Rockwall Youth Community OutReach program and Trusted World Organization.

If you have any questions regarding billing or project estimates, please email Tracie today!
