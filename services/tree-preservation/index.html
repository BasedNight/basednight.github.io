Tree Preservation

Tree preservation is the art and science of protecting valuable landscape and soil resources during new construction or site redevelopment. Landscape trees on residential and commercial properties contribute significantly to the aesthetic, functional and monetary values of the site. Unfortunately, these trees are often damaged so severely during construction that many decline and die within a few years. Sam Hill Tree Care can outline a specific tree preservation program to protect your trees from harmful construction practices that damage the roots, trunk or limbs of your beautiful trees.
